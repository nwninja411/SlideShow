The Horton Creek Collection

By Noah Walker

Horton Creek is a beatuiful hike located 20 miles from Payson Arizona. Horton Creek is part of the Mogollon Rim area and through the entire hike you are following a creek that has beatuiful photo opprotunities. In this collection you will have the opprotunity to look at some of the photos I captured during my time hiking Horton Creek. Enjoy!
